Map Legends

Green Deer

My map makes use of some easy to understand Legends related to the area. The first legend is the Green Deer which represents the Main area of the Serengeti National Park. The marker of the Green Deer points approximately to the center of the entire park as a whole, meaning from this point, most hotels and offices are easily accessible.

Hotels

During the making of my map, I've thoroughly scanned the Serengeti's best accomodations. I handpicked hotels and campsites that are close to the popular animal sites. The accomodations I have picked are also in a reasoable price range for those looking to spend a lot, or very little.

Map Legends

Animal Sites

This section does not include all the animal sites in the Seregeti, the park is too large for me to cover all of them. I've simply handpicked some popular sites which tourists visit, including WildBeast territory and spots where lion prides hang out. There are two types of legends in this section, the Orange Paw and the Blue Paw. Orange representing land animals, blue representing river sites where hippos, crocodiles and other water mammals can be spotted.
